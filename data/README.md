# HSK 4 Learning Platform

A comprehensive Chinese learning platform designed for HSK Level 4 students, featuring interactive dialogues, reading materials, and practice questions.

## Features

### For Learners
- **Interactive Dialogues**: Realistic conversations covering daily life, work, and travel
- **Reading Materials**: Short and long texts with comprehension questions
- **Practice Questions**: Fill-in-the-blanks and multiple-choice questions
- **Progress Tracking**: Visual progress bar and completion statistics
- **Toggle Features**: Show/hide pinyin and translations as needed
- **Responsive Design**: Works on desktop, tablet, and mobile devices

### For Administrators
- **Secure Admin Access**: Press Ctrl+Shift+A and enter key "140506"
- **Database Integration**: Connect to Supabase for live content management
- **Bulk Upload**: Upload learning materials via text files
- **Content Management**: Edit, delete, and add new content
- **User Tracking**: Monitor student progress and completion rates
